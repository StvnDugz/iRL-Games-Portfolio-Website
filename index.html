I.R.L. Games

I.R.L. Games is an independent game developer and publisher based in Cork, Ireland. Our company was founded in 2018 by game designer Steven Duggan. Since then, we have created En-Trance and ShinoBoy. Today, we are a team of four creative professionals, coming from a broad range of experience and education. We are hard at work on bringing En-Trance and ShinoBoy to more players - and on making new games.
