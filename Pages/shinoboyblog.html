ShinoBoy Releases Today


Febuary 21, 2020

Hi there,


ShinoBoy, our first major release since En-Trance, is out today.

It’s been a while since I’ve had the pleasure of penning a blog post – the last was when we launched revealed ShinoBoy. Well, we’re finally back with the release date. ShinoBoy is a single-player, narrative-driven platformer about climbing a mountain, and it’s launching today on PC, PS4 and Xbox One.

The ShinoBoy is on a mission to climb the mountain, and it won’t be easy. The mountain spans over 5 levels packed with hardcore platforming challenges and devious secrets.


Along the way the ShinoBoy will meet peculiar characters and confront his inner demons as he jumps, climbs, and air-dashes his way to the summit.

If you’re not looking for a challenge, try playing on Easy Difficulty, which lets you toggle some powerful accessibility features to tune the game’s difficulty to whatever suits you. You can give yourself extra air dashes, slow the game speed, and more. We certainly designed ShinoBoy to be a challenge, but we understand that every player is different and we want allow players from a wide range of skill levels to enjoy it.


ShinoBoy is a modern take on the classic action platformer. It’s a genre very close to our hearts, and we wanted to create something that pushes the genre – and our team – in a new direction. The result is a game that’s very personal and unlike anything else we’ve played.

We can’t wait to share it with you, and we hope you’ll enjoy your time. See you at the top!
