Contact Us

We do read everything that we receive, but due to the extensive amount of email inquiries in our mailbox we cannot guarantee that we will reply to each one. You are welcome to contact us, but before you write, please have a look at the following page for answers to the most common questions that we receive.

Before contacting us, please read this page. You will find answers to common inquiries and instructions on how to best reach us.


Press Inquries

For press inquiries please contact us at press@irlgames.com


Other Requests

Due to the high volume of inquiries, we are regrettably unable to answer all of them personally. However, we do read all of the emails we receive, and we will get back to you if we can help with your inquiry.


You would like to conduct an interview

Because we need to stay focused on development, we rarely participate in interviews. With this in mind, if you still want to inquire about conducting an interview, you are welcome to do so.


You want to share ShinoBoy or En-Trance footage

Permission granted. We own and control all rights to En-Trance and ShinoBoy and hereby grant you permission to share footage in the mentioned context. Please do not present the audio or visuals detached from one another. You are welcome to add your voice-over commentary. You can find additional supporting and useful materials at irlgames.com/press


You want to do a review of ShinoBoy

Please contact press@irlgames.com for review codes.

The PC, PS4 and Xbox One versions of En-Trance and ShinoBoy are available to download.


You want to work at I.R.L. Games

Find more information about vacancies and how to apply in our info@irlgames.com


You want to be an intern at I.R.L. Games

We rarely engage in internships as our resources are limited and focused. On rare occasions we may find a match with long-term internships (3 months+). We never take short term internships, work placements, job training or equivalent. Please direct your application to info@irlgames.com


You have a technical problem with En-Trance or ShinoBoy

For ShinoBoy related technical issues go to the support@irlgames.com for further assistance.

For En-Trance related technical issues go to the support@irlgames.com for assistance.

If you don’t find a solution, please write us at support@irlgames.com


You appreciate our work and want to express your feelings

Please send a mail; we will forward your mail to the whole team. You shouldn’t expect an answer, but you've made us very happy.


You want to do business with us

We get many proposals (and many good ones). However, we need to concentrate on continuing to make great games. With the small team that we have, the likelihood of doing a deal with anyone is small. That said, you are very welcome to reach out to us and we will get back to you if we see a potential fit.


You have read all of the above and just want to get in touch

Generally you want to contact info@irlgames.com but for technical support, you should reach out to support@irlgames.com For job-related inquiries, please find details on info@irlgames.com
