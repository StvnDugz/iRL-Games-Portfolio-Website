Celebrating our 1st anniversary


September 10, 2019

Today the team took a quick break from the development of our next game to celebrate One Year of I.R.L. Games. We were able to enjoy some ShinoBoy topped cupcakes throughout the day!

Our resident artist, Ana, created I.R.L. game-inspired artwork to share with our social community as part of the celebrations as well.

One of the highlights was also the opportunity to engage with our fans and friends, where we’ve heard what their favorite memories from playing En-Trance had been. We give thanks to all of our wonderful fans over the world for their birthday wishes! – the team at I.R.L. Games.
